P rocedure •
1) Go through the regulation documents of your respective programs, available on IIITD website (https://iiitd.ac.in/academics/resources)

2) Any doubts in the regulations may be clarified from your respective program admin(Admin-btech, admin-mtech, admin-phd).

3) Anything which is not mentioned in the regulation and being quoted by anyone should be cleared over email to avoid confusion (verbal confirmation will not be accepted)

4) Make sure to register your courses on ERP before the last date of add/drop. This selection will be final and cannot be changed later.

5) Make sure that you receive grades of all your courses. "I" grade is an Incomplete grade, which is automatically converted to a "F" grade if no grade is received till after one month of the declaration of results.

6) Check the graduation requirements mentioned in the regulation every semester to make sure that you are doing all the required courses and will graduate in time.
